# Appendix X. Interactional lexicon: terms, sources, and concordance-based exclusions. Each term was searched over the title and abstract of all 866 records. Layers are theory-derived and kept disjoint (terms shared by conversation analysis and the interface literature are assigned to the conversation-analytic layer). 'Records' is the number of records in which the term occurs; 'Records near a node word' is the subset in which it co-occurs with a conversation-family node word within the concordance window. Ambiguous terms were checked against their concordances and, where not used in the interactional sense, excluded (reason given). Per-layer record totals are not the sum of the term rows (a record may contain several terms from one layer); the record-level counts underlying the gradient figure are reported in Section 6.
